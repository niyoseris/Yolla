Yolla gets its energy from Gmail SMTP Server and fswebcam. Yolla is a very very simple security cam alternative written in Python.

Open or create a mail address [from address] and allow 3rd party applications can use it. ( https://support.google.com/a/answer/6260879 )

Connect your webcam to your pc or RasPi and run the script.

Install fswebcam

It will took a photo and send to your [to address] within about 15 seconds.

It's a base. You may modify it easily with any commands to do anything. Starting servo motors, opening water pumps, controlling lights, getting information about enviroment... anything. 

default command is:

/./:yolla/./
